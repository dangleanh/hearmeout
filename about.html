VISION
To create a bridge between the deaf community and the hearing.

By providing a platform for the public to be equipped with the necessary tools to ease the communication barrier between the hearing community and the deaf community.

MISSION

ABOUT HEAR ME OUT

We chose this topic because it is one that is very close to our group. Two of our members, Le Anh and Carina are from HI! Club (a Ngee Ann Polytechnic Co-Curriculum Activity), otherwise known as the Hearing Impaired Club. Both of them have been actively involved in events catered to the deaf community, and are not strangers to the sign language.

The concept behind our campaign is connecting members of our community by promoting sign language, in order to deconstruct the language barrier (and hence, the between the deaf and hearing. To do this, we created an app that allows individuals to translate hand signing into words and phrases, and vice versa. We also engaged the help of tutors who will be able to teach Hearing individuals sign language. Basic signings are introduced on our learn page.

We aim to encourage interaction between the deaf community and the hearing community. We believe that every individual has a story to tell, and hearing loss or lack thereof should not stand in the way of that.
