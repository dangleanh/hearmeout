OUR
MOBILE APP

MOTION SENSOR TRANLATOR

The app has a function which tracks the movement of the person the camera is pointing at. It captures motion of hand signing and translates it into words and phrases. User can type in words and phrases and the app will demonstrate the signing.

TRANSLATOR

The app can also act as a ‘visual’ translator, where you type in a word or phrase, and the app will translate it into sign language. A video or an image of how the word or phrase is signed will be shown on screen shortly.
